"MATTA EDUCTIONAL SOCUETY"

MATTA EDUCTIONAL SOCUETYwas established in Sattenapalli in 2001 under Educational Societies act XXI of 1860(1996) to propagate the educational need of backward area of palnadu. Since then it has been running very well. Our society has some aims and object.First of all is to establish,organize and run educational institutions for diffusion of useful knowledge and prepare the positive of all community for career in life.

This dream came to reality by establishing "SRI BABA GURUDEV DEGREE COLLEGE UNDER THIS SOCIETY" in 2005.
